Clinical Motivation

[cite_start]

Heart failure (HF) affects over 26 million people globally[cite: 96]. [cite_start]This project focuses on two high-risk patient groups: those with hypertrophic cardiomyopathy (HCM) and those affected by COVID-19[cite: 104, 106]. [cite_start]The goal is to enhance disease management and provide novel tools for better risk stratification[cite: 94].
